Nutrition

Eat more fruit, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid processed foods and drink plenty of water daily.

Exercise

Stay active for at least 30 minutes each day. Walking, cycling, or playing sports are great options.

Sleep

Good sleep helps the body recover. Aim for 7-9 hours of quality rest every night.

Mental Health

Practise mindfulness, take breaks, and talk to loved ones. A healthy mind is just as important as a healthy body.
